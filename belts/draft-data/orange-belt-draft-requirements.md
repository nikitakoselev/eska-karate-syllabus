The requirements for orange belt are these:
Kihon (basics):
1. Choku Zuki (straight punch - from natural stance)
2. Gyaku Zuki (reverse punch - stationary from front stance)
3. Oi Zuki (lunge punch - stepping in front stance)
4. Age Uke (rising block - stepping in front stance)
5. Ude Uke (outside block - stepping in front stance)
6. Uchi Uke (inside block - stepping in front stance)
7. Shuto Uke (knife hand block - stepping in front stance)
8. Mae Geri (front kick - steppin in front stance)
9. Yoko Geri Keage (side snap kick - stepping in side stance)
10. Yoko Geri Kekome (side thrust kick - steppin in side stance)

Kumite (Sparring)
Kihon Ippon Kumite - SET 1 (basic one step sparring - both sides)

Kata (forms)
Heian Shodan
